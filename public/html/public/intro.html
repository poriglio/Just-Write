Eventually, I will add functionality so that you can delete submissions and comments; edit your profiles; and edit submissions and comments. Until then, I hope you enjoy the site! Let minplynn@aol.com know if you find any bugs!

Just Write is just what it sounds like! It's a bare-bones site for writers to share their essays, stories, and poetry, with none of the distractions many other writing sites have. You won't find excessive images and video or social media here; communication is limited to comments on submissions, so that writers can focus on improving their work and helping others improve theirs.
